Media Bias Ratings

AllSides Media Bias Ratings™ make the political leanings of hundreds of media sources transparent so that you can get the full picture and think for yourself. AllSides has rated over 1,400 sources.

How We Rate Media Bias

We use multipartisan analysis to reflect the average view of Americans.

Center Doesn't Mean Better

Center-rated outlets may omit important perspectives.

Ratings Are Subject To Change

We frequently review media outlets to ensure our ratings are up-to-date.

Read Balanced News

Side-by-side coverage from media outlets on the left, right and center.

Submit A Source

If you search our ratings for a certain source and still don’t see it, email us.

Support Bias Ratings

If you value transparency, help fund our media bias research.

1.6k Shares

Unless otherwise noted, our bias ratings are based on online, written content, not TV or radio content.
